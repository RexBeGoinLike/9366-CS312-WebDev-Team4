Technological & Infrastructure Causes of Inequality

Technology and infrastructure are key to progress. Unequal access to internet, electricity, and transport deepens inequality between urban and rural communities.

Uneven Internet Access

Urban areas have faster internet, while many rural communities struggle with poor or no connectivity. Students and workers in remote provinces are left behind in the digital age.

Poor Transportation

Rural areas often lack reliable roads and public transport. This prevents access to schools, hospitals, and markets, isolating communities economically and socially.

Lack of Rural Electrification

Some barangays still lack stable electricity. Without power, healthcare, education, and local businesses cannot function effectively, widening the rural-urban divide.
