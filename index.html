Bridging the Gap: Understanding Inequality

The Philippines is one of Asia’s fastest-growing economies, yet millions remain in poverty. This site explores the causes, statistics, and solutions to inequality — and how we can create a society where growth is shared by everyone.

High Income Inequality

The richest 10% of Filipinos control more wealth than the poorest 50%. Unequal access to jobs, corruption, and disasters deepen the divide...
