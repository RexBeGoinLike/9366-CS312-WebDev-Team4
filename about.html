About TransparencyPH

TransparencyPH is a public platform designed to track major infrastructure projects across the Philippines. Our mission is to provide open access to information about projects that shape the nation’s future.

We categorize projects by status: Completed, In Progress, Planning, and Delayed. Information includes location, contractors, budget, and timelines.

By offering this data in a simple and transparent format, we aim to empower citizens, researchers, and policymakers to stay informed about national development.
