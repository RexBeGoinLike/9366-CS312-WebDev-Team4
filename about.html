Welcome to THRIVE

THRIVE is a movement born from the belief that inequality — whether in income, education, health, or culture — should never define our nation. Inspired by global principles of human dignity and equity, we work to empower marginalized communities across the Philippines to live with justice, opportunity, and voice.

Our Mission

To build an inclusive Philippines where every individual — regardless of region, gender, ethnicity, or social status — has equal access to basic rights, services, and opportunities to thrive.

Core Values

  • Equity: Ensuring fair access to resources and opportunities.
  • Respect: Honoring diverse cultures, traditions, and voices.
  • Empowerment: Building capacity in communities to claim their rights.
  • Accountability: Advocating for transparent policies and governance.
  • Solidarity: Working together — grassroots to government, local to global.

What We Do

Community Education Initiatives

We partner with schools and indigenous communities to create culturally sensitive learning materials, support teachers, and build learning hubs in remote areas.

Healthcare Outreach Programs

Mobile clinics, maternal health campaigns, and partnerships with local health workers bring essential care closer to underserved rural communities.

Advocacy & Legal Support

We assist indigenous groups in securing ancestral land titles, push for equal pay policies, and raise awareness about gender-based discrimination laws.

Environmental Equity

We work to protect ancestral lands from destructive mining, strengthen climate resilience for vulnerable communities, and promote sustainable resource use.

Voices from the Ground

“Our Land, Our Voice” – The Lumad Fight Back

Lumad elders from the Cordillera region recount decades of displacement due to mining. The community now monitors all proposed mining permits, files legal cases, and is restoring degraded ancestral lands with native tree planting.
