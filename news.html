Philippine Government Expands Social Amelioration Program

Published: January 2025

The Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) announced the expansion of its aid program to reach 5 million more low-income families. The move aims to ease inequality worsened by inflation and rising food prices.

NGOs Push for Inclusive Education

Published: November 2024

Local NGOs are calling for better funding for public schools in rural areas, citing that lack of resources widens the education gap between rich and poor students.
